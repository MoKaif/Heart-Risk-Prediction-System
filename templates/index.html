Categories of Heart Disease

1. Angina

Angina or Angina Pectoris occurs when area of heart muscle does not get enough oxygen which can result in heart diseases such as Coronary Heart Disease

2. Arrhythmia

Arrhythmia is an irregular heartbeat that occurs when electrical signals that coordinate the heart's beats don't work properly.

3. Atrial Fibrillation

Atrial Fibrillation is irregular and rapid heart beat that leads to clots in heart.

4. Myocardial infarction

A myocardial infarction or commonly known as heart attack is a condition caused by a lack of blood flow to the heart muscle

5. Heart Failure

Heart failure also known as congestive heart failure, occurs when the heart muscle doesn't pump blood as well as it should.
